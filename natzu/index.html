Charting Donald Trump's Misogyny

What exactly has Donald Trump said about women?

Donald Trump is the current president of the United States and one of the most controversial. Trump has a history of unregulated comments and insensitive slurs. This infographic focuses solely on comments he has made about his female colleagues, female professionals he has shared interactions with and his female relatives that are deemed as derogatory and mysogistic according to several sources.

Scroll to the left and journey through Trump’s comments about his female colleagues within the last 30 years. The information within each year is placed in alphabetical order by the female's last name.

About this Data Set

The sources used for this visualization were Vox Media, The Week, BBC and The Telegraph. There is an extensive set of comments that have not been placed in this timeline due to time constraints and the enormity of the data set.
